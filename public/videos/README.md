# Video Assets

## Hero Video

Place your hero background video here as: `hero-construction.mp4`

### Recommended Video Specs:
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or higher
- **Duration**: 10-30 seconds (looping)
- **Theme**: Construction, water infrastructure, concrete work, or industrial projects
- **File Size**: Keep under 10MB for optimal loading

### Free Video Sources:
- Pexels: https://www.pexels.com/videos/
- Pixabay: https://pixabay.com/videos/
- Coverr: https://coverr.co/

### Search Keywords:
- "construction site"
- "concrete pouring"
- "industrial infrastructure"
- "water treatment plant"
- "tunnel construction"
- "dam construction"

Currently, the site falls back to the static hero image (`/images/hero-bg.jpg`) if no video is found.
